🚅 Traveling around the world

  1. Maharashtra

    • Raigad Fort



      Raigad Fort, a historic hill fort and former capital of the Maratha Empire, offers historical significance and scenic beauty.

    • Shirdi Temple



      Visiting Shirdi offers spiritual peace and a connection to Sai Baba.

  2. Kerala

    • Kerala



      Kerala is known for houseboats in Alleppey, tea gardens in Munnar, and cultural richness in Kochi.

    • Kochi


      Kochi offers a mix of culture, history, and natural beauty.
